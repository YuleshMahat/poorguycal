# Privacy Policy for PoorGuy Cal

_Last updated: June 2026_


## 1. Overview

PoorGuy Cal is a calorie tracking and nutrition planning application that allows users to create meals, add ingredients, log food intake, set nutrition goals, and view daily dashboards.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to operate primarily on your device. Most data is stored locally and is not transmitted to external servers.

### 2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect the following information when you use the App:

- Name (if provided for account or entitlement linking)
- Email address (used for purchase/entitlement association via RevenueCat)
- Meals, ingredients, and food logs you create
- Nutrition goals and settings

### 2.2 Photos and Media

Photos taken or uploaded in the App are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or access your photos on external servers.

### 2.3 Purchase Information

Payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We may receive non-financial transaction data (e.g., purchase status) via third-party services such as RevenueCat to manage entitlements.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only to:

- Provide core app functionality (food logging, meal creation, dashboards)
- Save and display your nutrition data
- Link purchases to your account or device entitlements
- Improve app performance and user experience

We do not use your data for advertising or behavioral tracking.

## 4. Data Storage

All personal nutrition data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (e.g., React Native Async Storage). You are responsible for managing your device data and backups. Deleting the App will remove locally stored data unless otherwise backed up by your device.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We only share limited information with trusted third-party services necessary for app functionality, including:

- **RevenueCat** — for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **Apple App Store / Google Play Billing** — for processing payments

These providers are subject to their own privacy policies.

## 6. Data Retention

Since most data is stored locally, we do not retain user data on our servers. Purchase-related identifiers may be retained by RevenueCat as required for entitlement management.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

PoorGuy Cal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, since most data is stored locally on your device, security also depends on your device’s security settings.

## 9. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **RevenueCat** — subscription and purchase management
- **Apple App Store / Google Play Billing** — payment processing

Each service has its own privacy policy.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access your data
- Delete your data (by removing the app or clearing local storage)
- Request information about data handling

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the App or on the App Store listing.
